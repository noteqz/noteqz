Good to know

Questions, answered

Which classes is NoteQz for?

NoteQz is built specifically for Class 9 & 10 — Science, Mathematics and Social Science, mapped chapter by chapter to the school syllabus.

Is it really free?

Yes. You can learn and practise for free — unlock lessons by earning gems or watching a short rewarded ad. Pro removes the ads and every gate for unlimited access.

Does it work in Hindi?

Fully. Every lesson, question and summary is authored in both English and हिन्दी, and you can switch medium anytime in Settings.

Do I need to be online?

You need a connection to load new lessons and past papers. Your progress, streaks and performance analysis are computed and kept on your own device.

Can I delete my account and data?

Anytime, from inside the app — Settings → Delete account removes your account and associated data. The full detail is in our Privacy Policy.
